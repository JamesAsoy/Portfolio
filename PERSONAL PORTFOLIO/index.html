About Me

I am James David B. Asoy, a 3rd Year BSIT student, and I had the valuable opportunity to join the educational tour in Cebu and Bohol. This experience allowed me to learn beyond the classroom by visiting various technological, industrial, and cultural sites that enhanced my understanding of real-world applications of information technology.
